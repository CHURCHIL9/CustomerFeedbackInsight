Service: Customer Feedback Insights Analysis

What the service provides:

• Automatic grouping of feedback into key themes
• Identification of recurring issues and opportunities
• Clean Excel reports with insights and charts
• Executive summary for decision makers

Typical Use Cases

• Customer satisfaction surveys
• Product feedback
• Service reviews
• Internal employee feedback
• Market research surveys

Deliverables

Clients receive a professional Excel report containing:
• Executive summary
• Theme distribution charts
• Key insights
• Business recommendations

This allows organizations to quickly understand what customers are saying and make data-driven decisions.
