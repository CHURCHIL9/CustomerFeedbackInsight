Customer Feedback Insights – Key Findings

Dataset: Sample E-Commerce Customer Feedback
Number of responses analyzed: 300

Main Themes Identified

1. Delivery Issues
   Many customers mentioned delayed deliveries and lack of tracking updates.

2. Product Quality Concerns
   Some feedback highlighted damaged packaging and defective products.

3. Customer Support Experience
   Customers appreciate fast responses but dislike long waiting times during peak hours.

4. Website User Experience
   Users suggested improvements in search functionality and easier checkout.

Key Business Opportunities

• Improve delivery communication and tracking updates
• Strengthen product quality checks before shipping
• Expand customer support availability during peak periods
• Simplify checkout process on the website

Value of This Analysis

Instead of manually reading hundreds of responses, the NLP system automatically groups feedback into themes and provides actionable insights for decision makers.
