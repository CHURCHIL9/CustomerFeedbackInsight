CASE STUDY
Healthcare Survey Feedback Analysis

Project Overview

A community healthcare survey collected 300 open-ended responses from respondents describing the challenges they face when accessing healthcare services.

Manually reading and categorizing hundreds of responses can take several days and may miss recurring patterns.

This project demonstrates how Natural Language Processing (NLP) can automatically analyze large volumes of qualitative feedback and produce structured insights.

Dataset

• Total responses analyzed: 300
• Data type: Open-ended survey responses
• Topic: Healthcare access challenges

Methodology

The analysis pipeline performs several automated steps:

• Text preprocessing and cleaning
• Sentiment analysis
• Sentence embeddings using transformer models
• Clustering responses into thematic groups
• Keyword extraction for each theme
• Automated insight generation

Technologies Used

Python with machine learning libraries including:

• SentenceTransformers
• scikit-learn
• NLTK

Key Themes Identified

The system automatically identified multiple recurring themes in the survey responses, including:

• Long waiting times at healthcare facilities
• High cost of consultation and medication
• Long distances to health centers
• Medicine stock shortages
• Negative staff attitudes
• Transportation barriers

Key Insights

The analysis revealed several major issues affecting healthcare access.

• Transportation and distance barriers were mentioned frequently by respondents.

• Long waiting times generated strong negative sentiment among patients.

• Cost of healthcare services remains a major challenge for many respondents.

• Medicine stock shortages were also reported as a recurring issue.

Business Value

Instead of manually reading hundreds of responses, the NLP system automatically groups feedback into themes and highlights the most critical issues.

This allows organizations to:

• Quickly understand major challenges faced by communities
• Prioritize service improvements
• Make faster data-driven decisions
• Reduce manual analysis time

Deliverables

The system produces a structured Excel report containing:

• Executive summary for decision makers
• Theme analysis table
• Charts visualizing major issues
• Key insights
• Strategic recommendations
• Full categorized response dataset
